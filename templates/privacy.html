Privacy Policy

At {{ app_name }}, your privacy is our top priority. We are committed to protecting your personal and financial data.

We do NOT collect, store, or transmit your personal or financial data to any external servers.
How Your Data is Stored
Local Storage Mode (Default)
  • Your data stays on YOUR device - All portfolio data is stored exclusively in your web browser's localStorage.
  • No server transmission - In local mode, your data never leaves your browser. The backend only performs calculations when you request them, using data you send in each request.
  • Stateless architecture - Our server does not store any user data. Each calculation request is independent and your data is not retained.
  • Browser-specific - Your data is tied to the specific browser on your device. Clearing browser data will remove your portfolio data.
  • Export for backup - We recommend regularly exporting your data to Excel for backup purposes.
Firebase Cloud Mode (Optional)
  • Your own Firebase account - If you choose Firebase mode, data is stored in YOUR personal Firebase Firestore database using YOUR credentials.
  • You control the data - We do not have access to your Firebase project or data. You provide your own service account credentials.
  • Direct connection - The app connects directly to your Firebase project; we act only as an interface.
What We Do NOT Do
  • We do NOT collect personal information
  • We do NOT track your financial data
  • We do NOT store data on our servers
  • We do NOT sell or share any data
  • We do NOT use cookies for tracking
  • We do NOT have analytics or telemetry
  • We do NOT require account registration
  • We do NOT have access to your passwords
Third-Party Services

{{ app_name }} may connect to the following external APIs for real-time data (optional features):

  • MFAPI.in - For fetching mutual fund NAV data (Indian mutual funds)
  • Metals.live - For fetching current gold and silver prices

These API calls only fetch public market data and do not transmit any of your personal or portfolio information.

Your Responsibilities
  • Backup your data - Since data is stored locally, please export to Excel regularly.
  • Secure your device - Ensure your device and browser are secure to protect your data.
  • Firebase credentials - If using Firebase mode, keep your service account credentials secure and never share them.
Contact

If you have any questions about this privacy policy or our data practices, please reach out to us.
